DIKSHA APP

Diksha is an Indian government app offering free, high-quality digital learning resources for students, teachers, and parents across all grades and subjects. It provides textbooks, videos, quizzes, and interactive content aligned with various state and national curricula. Available in multiple languages, Diksha supports personalized learning and offline access, making education accessible anytime, anywhere.
